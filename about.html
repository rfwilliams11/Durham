Robert Durham Architecture

"Architecture is a matter of understanding how things should work and be built."

Robert Durham Architecture is a small office located in Central Texas providing comprehensive professional architectural solutions to clients seeking responsive planning and design services. We understand that design is only one piece of a project’s success and strive to deliver high-quality and value-based service for all projects.

Beginning his own practice in 1987, Robert Durham’s early experience primarily focused on commercial projects, including hospitals, educational facilities, office buildings, and golf clubhouses. Over the past few years the practice worked primarily on single family residential designs. Clients include homeowners, investors, and speculative builders focused on high-end design with maximum end-value. A residence represents a significant investment to the homeowner and is a long term commitment to their future.

With over 33 years of experience, Robert Durham Architecture possesses the experience to implement the desired elegance and sustainability in any design approach. Being a small office, Robert Durham is the primary contact for all projects. For every endeavor we are committed to elegance and simplicity in design to bring joy and comfort to the client.
