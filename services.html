Services

Robert Durham Architecture provides comprehensive professional architectural solutions to clients seeking responsive and reliable planning and design services. We understand that great design is only one component of a project’s success and strive to deliver high-quality, value-based, and timely service for projects both large and small.
Site Analysis

Engagement at the very earliest stages of the project with site identification and planning allows us to understand and optimize the flow and functionality of the site for maximum efficiency and value.

Project Assessment

A thorough understanding of a project’s scope and possibilities that properly aligns with client needs and expectations will ensure a successful undertaking.

Architectural Services

Extensive architectural knowledge and decades of design experience translates into an effective building process. This process creates more accurate and detailed plans that enhance efficiency at every level of the project.

Product Research

Expert understanding of the architectural and design product marketplace will deliver the highest quality and value to ensure project success.

Contract Negotations

Getting to a shared understanding of the project scope, necessary services, and risk management for all parties is crucial to a design’s outcome. Using the contract as a project framework can go a long way toward improving both process and result.

Contract Compliance Assurance

Continual assessment of contract terms and priorities establishes a foundation of quality and efficiency to propel operational success.
